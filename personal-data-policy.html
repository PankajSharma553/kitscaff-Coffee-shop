Personal Data Policy

Kits Caff is committed to protecting the personal data of our customers, website visitors, and business partners. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information in accordance with Canadian privacy laws, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)

1. What Personal Data We Collect

We may collect personal data when you interact with our website, visit our caf√©, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and any details provided when subscribing to our newsletter or contacting us.
  • Transaction Data: Payment method (we do not store full credit card details), purchase history, and order preferences.
  • Website & Device Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior through cookies and analytics.
  • Social Media Interactions: If you engage with our social media pages, we may collect your username and interactions with our content.

2. How We Use Personal Data

We use personal data to:

  • Process orders and provide receipts for purchases.
  • Respond to inquiries and customer service requests.
  • Send marketing communications (only if you have opted in).
  • Improve website functionality and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

3. How We Protect Personal Data

We implement appropriate security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. These measures include encryption, access controls, and secure servers.

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes stated or as required by law. When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

4. Sharing of Personal Data

We do not sell personal data to third parties. However, we may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help process payments, send emails, or provide analytics services.
  • Legal & Regulatory Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights and property.

All third-party service providers are required to handle personal data securely and in compliance with privacy laws.

5. Your Rights Over Personal Data

Under Canadian privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and request a copy.
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Request deletion your personal data where applicable.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@kitscaff.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies to enhance user experience and gather analytics. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies, though some site features may not function properly.

7. Third-Party Websites

Our website may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
