Privacy Policy

At Kits Caff, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information in compliance with Canadian privacy laws, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information when you interact with our website, visit our café, or communicate with us:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and any details provided when subscribing to our newsletter or contacting us.
  • Transaction Information: Details related to purchases made at our café, including payment method (we do not store full credit card details).
  • Website Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Social Media Interactions: If you engage with our social media pages, we may collect your username and interactions with our content.

2. How We Use Your Information

The personal information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To process transactions and provide receipts for purchases.
  • To respond to inquiries and customer support requests.
  • To send marketing communications, such as promotions or updates (only if you have opted in).
  • To improve our website experience through analytics and user feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may work with trusted third parties to process payments, send email communications, or provide analytics services. These providers are required to keep your information confidential.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights and property, we may disclose information to law enforcement or regulatory authorities.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and gather analytics data. You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies, but this may affect certain functionalities of our website.

5. Data Security and Retention

We implement security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, and loss. We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Under Canadian privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in emails.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@kitscaff.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies before providing personal information.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle personal information, please reach out to:

Email: info@kitscaff.com

Address: 2325 West 4th Ave, Vancouver, BC
